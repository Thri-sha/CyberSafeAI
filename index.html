📝 Cyber Security Quiz

🚨 Latest Scam Alerts

  • ⚠️ Never share your OTP with anyone.
  • ⚠️ Beware of fake bank calls.
  • ⚠️ Don't scan unknown QR codes.
  • ⚠️ Verify UPI payment requests before paying.
  • ⚠️ Don't click suspicious email or SMS links.

🛡️ Cyber Safety Tips

  • ✅ Never share your OTP or banking PIN.
  • ✅ Use strong passwords with uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols.
  • ✅ Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
  • ✅ Verify websites before entering personal information.
  • ✅ Keep your software and antivirus updated.
  • ✅ Don't click suspicious email or SMS links.
  • ✅ Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking transactions.
  • ✅ Always log out from shared devices.
